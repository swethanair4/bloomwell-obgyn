Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to make an appointment?

No referral is necessary. You can book a consultation directly through our website without needing prior approval.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers. Visit our insurance page or contact our billing team for details.

How can I access my test results?

Log in to your BloomWell patient portal using your secure credentials to view test results and visit summaries.

Are virtual appointments available?

Yes, we offer secure virtual consultations using HIPAA-compliant video platforms for your convenience and safety.
